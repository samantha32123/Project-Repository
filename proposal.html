Project Proposal

What is the website about?

This website is about the history of the Babson College Women's Basketball program.

What is the objective of the website?

The objective of this website is to analyze the historic coaching, teams, and games that developed the program that stands today. Throughout photos, videos, and archives, this website will highlight the foundation of Babson Basketball.

Who is the target audience?

The target audience for this website is current players, future recruits and Babson alumni that are interested in knowing more about the program.

What content will be included on this website

I plan to construct a well-organized layout that hosts different pages with varying types of information. For example:

  • Photos
  • Videos
  • Coaching History
  • Championship teams
  • Personal Testimonials
